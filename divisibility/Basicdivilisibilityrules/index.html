Basic Divisibility Rules

Here are the most basic divisibility rules. Do note that if you have a divisibility rule for a number like 12, the number must work for both the divisibility rule of 3 and 4

Divisibility by 2

The number must end in 0 , 2 , 4 , 6 or 8

Divisibility by 3

The sum of digits must be divisible by 3

Divisibility by 4

The last two digits must be divisible by 4

Divisibility by 5

The last digit must be 0 or 5

Divisibility by 7

This one is more complicated. Take the last digit and subtract two times of it from the rest of the number

There is also another method, but this is the same as divisibility by 11 or 13 so go check those out instead.

Divisibility by 8

The last 3 digits must be divisible by 8

Divisibility by 9

The sum of digits must be divisible by 9

Divisibility by 11

They are in the form of abcabcabc...abc

Divisibility by 13

They are in the form of abcabcabc...abc
