An ant can move in 3 different directions on a 5 x 5 x 5 cube. How many ways are there for the ant to move from one end of the cube to the other?(You can use a calculator)

The ant needs 15 movements to reach from one end to the other. Therefore the ant can move to the other end in 15C5 x 10C5 x 5C5 ways, or 756756 ways.
