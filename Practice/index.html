There is a wall in construction. Group A can finish the wall in 6 days. Group B can finish the wall in 3 days. If Group A and B work together, how long will it take for them to finish the wall?

The rate at which group A builds the wall is 1/6. The rate at which group B builds the wall is 1/3. The rate at which group A and B build the wall = 1/3 + 1/6 = 1/2. It takes 2 days
